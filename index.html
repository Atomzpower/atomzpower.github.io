About Us

Atomz Power is a startup working towards creating a smart, reliable and yet affordable EV Charging Infrastructure powered by Renewable Power.

Kiran and Praveen are the co-founders coming from Automotive background and have experienced the 4W and 2W EVs since 2015. We identified the hurdles and awful experiences faced by the EV users when compared to Petrol/diesel vehicle owner. We have designed the hardware and software platform from ground up to provide a premium user experience in EV charging by giving attention to intricacies of the system.

Our team is experienced in building the finest EV chargers in the country. Our mentors from the Netherlands guide us in implementing the latest technology on EV charging and navigating us through roadblocks with their experience in EV charging since 2009.
